FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is this extension free?

Yes, Chat Exporter for Grok is completely free to use with no hidden costs or subscriptions.

Does it work with all Grok conversations?

Yes! The extension works on grok.com and integrates seamlessly with all your existing and new conversations.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. The extension processes everything locally in your browser. We never send your chat data to any external server.

Do I need a Google account to export to Google Docs?

Yes, for Google Docs export you'll need to sign in with your Google account so the document can be saved to your Drive. Word and PDF exports work without signing in.

Can I export only part of a conversation?

Yes! You can select specific messages to export instead of the entire conversation. Perfect for saving only the parts that matter to you.
